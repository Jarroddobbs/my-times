Mother of Three Buys Tuna Steak at Phish Concert. Leaves, "Feeling Funny."

MONUMENT VALLEY, AZ - It was supposed to be a fun night out for mother of three, Janet Johnson. She had purchased tickets for the Phish Concert last Saturday evening, and attended with two friends. The venue of the concert was a local eatery in the downtown area which also hosts live music on the weekends. Janet ordered the tuna steak which she claims was recommended to her by her waiter. While the taste of the tuna steak left something to be desired, Janet says it didn't raise any red flags to alert her that something may not be right with the delicacy. It wasn't until the band was into their second set that Janet realised something 'fishy' was going on.

"I felt my stomach start to turn, and had to run to the restroom immediately. My friends and I left shortly afterwards," says Janet about the incident.

While Janet seems to have made a full recovery, she wants the resturaunt to take a closer look at the food they are serving their customers. We contacted the eatery in hopes of a statement, but our calls have not been returned.

Janet's friends are both vegan, so they were not affected by the 'fishy' food.

Cats in Control-Is It a Thing?

"A cat's meow actually contains high frequency vibrations that affect our brains in ways otherwise unknown," says Dr. Science. "In fact, those vibrations may trigger a need in us to fulfill whatever the cat is asking for."

If you think that sounds silly, just ask a cat owner.
